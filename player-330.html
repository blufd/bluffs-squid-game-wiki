History

Red Light, Green Light

At the start of the game, he targeted another contestant, Player 025. He threw a pocket of sand at him, angering 025 who then tried to escape. Being much faster, 330 caught up and pushed him repeatedly until he was eliminated. 330 then passed the game.

Dalgona

330 successfully passed the Dalgona challenge with a star shape. During this game, he made his first friend, Player 028, and they became buddies for the next event.

Lights Out

He and Player 028 grouped up, climbing on top of a bunk bed where they remained safely for the entire duration of the game.

Tug of War

In a cruel twist of fate, 330 and 028 were placed on opposing teams in the first round. Both were upset by this, and the match turned into a long, hard-fought battle. Ultimately, 330's team emerged victorious, eliminating his friend.

Jump Rope

330 chose to go last, a risky but successful strategy that saw him make it to the other side. This game wiped out half of the remaining contestants, leaving only four players.

Glass Bridge

He navigated the Glass Bridge carefully and was one of the two players, along with Player 114, to make it to the end.

Squid Game

In the final game, 330 faced off against Player 114. He dominated the match, flawlessly defeating his opponent to become the winner of the 30th Squid Game.

Post-Game

After winning the final round, 330 was awarded the prize money and transported back home.
