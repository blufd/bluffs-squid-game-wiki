History

Red Light, Green Light

In Red Light, Green Light, he witnessed the first deaths of many players. He then saw Player 275 push Player 150, causing Player 150 to be eliminated. This infuriated Player 197, so he went to avenge Player 150. He fought Player 275 and successfully eliminated him.

Dalgona

Passed easily; chose the triangle shape.

Lights Out

He grabbed a bottle and, with his buddy (Player 218), went to the men's bathroom to hide. However, there was already someone there, and Player 218 began attacking another player who entered. The people in the bathroom were hostile, leading to Player 197 and Player 218 sustaining many injuries while brawling with several others. In the last 10 seconds of Lights Out, Player 218 made the mistake of running out of the bathroom. He was jumped by other players because he was so injured, making him an easy target and resulting in his death. This deeply affected Player 197.

Tug of War

Not much happened, but he passed with his team.

Jump Rope

He also passed, staying quiet throughout the game.

Glass Bridge

Player 197 successfully passed this game.

Mingle

In Mingle, he was supposed to be eliminated in the first round. There were 8 people (when only 3 were needed per group), and Player 197 grouped up with Player 312 to head to a room. They entered a room thinking there were three of them, but the other group of three went to the door next to theirs. Player 197 and Player 312 were devastated, as the other group found a door as well. Player 312 accepted his fate, but Player 197 went outside and closed the door on him. He confronted the guards face-to-face before the timer ended, grabbed a guard's MP5, and shot several guards. He then grabbed magazines and another MP5. He quickly ran back to his door and gave Player 312 the second MP5. They fought off the guards together, and since the door was bulletproof, the guards couldn't do much. The guards evacuated the other 6 players and held the door. Player 197 and Player 312 kept fighting but eventually ran out of ammo. They surrendered, but the guards shot them anyway.

Death

Shot and killed by guards after surrendering during the game Mingle.
